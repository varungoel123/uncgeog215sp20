Google Search Tips For Spatial Data

If you’re trying to find spatial data, try a search string that is extremely detailed such as, study area gis data download. One semi-important observation that I have made is the importance of including the word download in your search string. For example, you could search for, botswana gis data download.

If you’ve already found spatial data and are trying to locate tabular data for a particular subject, use a search string with the word table and the geographic resolution (unit) of your spatial data included, e.g., study area topic unit table. For example, you could search for, england vaccination local authorities table.
